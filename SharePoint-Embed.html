The 4 Knowledge Domains (8-Day Allocation)

The 8-day sprint is balanced across four critical areas, ensuring proper focus on the high-risk security, critical user and device operational processes and backup systems.

The schedule allocates two days to each major domain: M365 Admin, Security, Backup/DR, and Critical Operational Handoffs (Kickoff & Final Sign-off).

The 8-Day Phased Schedule

A focused, day-by-day sequence to build knowledge logically from access and identity right through to disaster recovery and sign-off.

DAY 1

Kickoff & Access

Environment map review, credential exchange, and documentation access.

DAY 2

M365: Identity

Entra ID, user lifecycle management, and MFA/Conditional Access policies.

DAY 3

M365: Data & Comms

Exchange mail flow, SharePoint sharing configuration, and Teams review.

DAY 4

Security I: Endpoint/EDR

EDR solution console, alert handling, and standard remediation playbooks.

DAY 5

Security II: MDM & Email

Device Management (Intune) policies and email gateway quarantine release process.

DAY 6

Management Environment Deployment

Package and deploy Datto RMM via Intune.

DAY 7

Backups & Disaster Recovery

Review Backups & DR Runbook, RTOs, and a full system restore simulation exercise.

DAY 8

Ops Handoff & Sign-off

Ticketing and vendor management transfer. Formal KT Completion Sign-off.
